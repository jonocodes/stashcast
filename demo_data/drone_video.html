About QuickShots

DJI QuickShots are pre-programmed flight patterns that allow users to capture cinematic aerial footage with just a tap. The drone automatically performs complex maneuvers while keeping the subject in frame, making it easy to create professional-looking videos.

QuickShot Modes Include:

  • Dronie - Flies backward and upward, keeping the subject centered
  • Circle - Circles around the subject
  • Helix - Spirals upward around the subject
  • Rocket - Ascends with the camera pointing downward
  • Boomerang - Flies in an oval path around the subject
  • Asteroid - Flies backward and upward, then descends while creating a panorama
