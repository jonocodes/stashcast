About Pecha Kucha

PechaKucha (Japanese: ペチャクチャ) is a presentation style in which 20 slides are shown for 20 seconds each (6 minutes and 40 seconds in total). The format keeps presentations concise and fast-paced, making it an engaging way to share ideas, projects, and creative work.

This video version includes visual slides synchronized with the presentation.
